ARGATO

Argato is a family-owned mezcal whose agave grows in Guerrero, Mexico. A state off the country's western coast known for being a cultural melting pot, its biodiversity, tropical weather, and silver production. The brand is a tribute to the love for mezcal, the Landa family legacy, and the region it comes from; therefore, it features hints that tell a story. Starting with the name derived from the word "Argento", the Spanish scientific term used to refer to silver, and its periodic table symbol "Ag47", 47 standing for silver's atomic number.
